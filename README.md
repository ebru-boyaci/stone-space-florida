# Stone Space Miami

This repository contains the source for the **Stone Spaces** company website in the United States — the brand’s digital home for natural stone and surface solutions.

## About

Stone Spaces is the product showcase, company story, and customer-facing site for the U.S. market.

## What this site highlights

- **Kitchen styles** — curated looks and layouts customers can explore.
- **Materials in use** — surfaces, stone, and finishes actually specified in those kitchens.
- **Renovations and new layouts** — before/after and revised plans so visitors can see transformed spaces and updated room arrangements.
